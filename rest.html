Mexico

The mexican culture is expressed throughout the neighborhood of Bushwick. The colors of the Mexican flag, green white and red are shown through the Mexican taquerias and food trucks that surround Bushwick. Murals of Mexican artists such as Frida Kahlo are spray painted onto the buildings of Bushwick. Street vendors sell traditional mexican food like churros, elotes and horchata. Even the park I grew up in “Maria Hernandez Park” was named after a Mexican activist.

Ecuador

The people in the area of Bushwick where I live are predominantly Ecuadorian. Ecuadorian food trucks and food vendors usually surround our local catholic church, St.Brigid every Sunday.

Puerto Rico

In areas of Bushwick like Knickerbocker avenue and Myrtle avenue the population of Puerto Ricans are the highest. Puerto Rican street art is mainly seen in these areas. Reggaeton (originated from Puerto Rico) and Puerto Rican Salsa music is usually heard throughout Bushwick.
