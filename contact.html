Explore Bushwick, Brooklyn

Hi! My name is Kimberly Prieto and I am a student at Rochester Institute of Technology. Before moving to Rochester I was born and raised in Bushwick, Brooklyn. Bushwick, Brooklyn holds a very special place in my heart because it influenced me to become the artist I am today.

Bushwick, Brooklyn is a predominantly low-income neighborhood located in New York City. Bushwick is well known for their Mexican taquerias and graffiti street art. Bushwick is walking distance from Queens and only a 20 minute train ride from Manhattan. Come take a look at my neighborhood!
